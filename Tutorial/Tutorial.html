Registration on the Platform
  1. Enter your email address: In the "Email" field, provide your active email address (e.g., example@gmail.com).
  2. Create a password: In the "Password" field, enter a strong password (at least 8 characters, including letters, numbers, and special characters).
  3. Confirm the code from your email:
    • Go to your email inbox.
    • Find the email with the confirmation code from the platform.
    • Copy the code and paste it into the "Confirmation Code" field on the website.
  4. Enter the referral code (mandatory): If you have a referral code, enter it in the "Referral Code" field.
  5. Complete the registration:
    • Click the "Register" button.
    • After successful registration, you will be automatically redirected to the login page.
Logging into Your Account
  1. Enter your email address: On the login page, in the "Email" field, enter the email you used during registration.
  2. Enter your password: In the "Password" field, enter the password you created during registration.
  3. Log into your account:
    • Click the "Login" button.
    • Upon successful login, you will be redirected to the main page of your account.
Password Reset
  1. Enter your email address: On the "Password Reset" page, enter the email associated with your account.
  2. Receive the code via email:
    • Go to your email inbox.
    • Find the email with the password reset code.
    • Copy the code.
  3. Confirm the code:
    • Paste the received code into the "Confirmation Code" field on the website.
    • Click the "Confirm" button.
  4. Enter a new password: In the window that appears, enter a new password in the "New Password" field.
  5. Repeat the new password: In the "Repeat Password" field, enter the new password again to confirm.
  6. Save the changes:
    • Click the "Change Password" button.
    • After successfully changing the password, you can log into your account using the new password.

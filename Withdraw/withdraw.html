What is a BCS Withdrawal?

Ready to take your crypto out of the BCS network? A BCS withdrawal is like opening your futuristic piggy bank and sending your coins to your personal wallet! It’s a quick and secure way to transfer your funds back to your own BSC address, so you can use them anywhere in the crypto universe. Let’s get started!

How to Withdraw in BCS Network

  1. Enter your BSC address in the field above (make sure it’s correct!).
  2. Specify the amount you want to withdraw (e.g., 0.5 BNB).
  3. Click the "Withdraw" button to submit your request.
  4. Wait for the confirmation message: "Your request has been received and is under review.".
  5. Our team will review your request, and the funds will be sent to your BSC address (this usually takes 1-3 business days).
  6. Check your wallet to confirm the funds have arrived!

What are BCS Transactions?

Curious about your crypto journey in the BCS network? This page lets you peek into all your transactions — deposits, withdrawals, and more! It’s like a digital diary of your funds, showing you every move your crypto has made. Pick a date range and dive into your transaction history!

Understanding Your Transaction Table

Here’s what each column in the table means:

  • Date: The date when the transaction took place (e.g., 2025-03-15).
  • Type: The type of transaction — either "Deposit" (you added funds) or "Withdrawal" (you took funds out).
  • Amount: The amount of cryptocurrency involved in the transaction (e.g., 1.2 BNB).
  • Address: The wallet address involved in the transaction (either your BCS deposit address or the external address you withdrew to).
  • Status: The current status of the transaction — "Completed" (done) or "Pending" (still being processed).
